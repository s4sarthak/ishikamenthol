About Us

Ishika Menthol is a leading manufacturer and supplier of natural Menthol IP grade Crystals and Powder, crude menthol oil, and other essential oils. Established in 2003 by Mr. Manoj Gupta, the company has grown to become a preferred menthol supplier, driven by a clear vision and a dedicated team. With a decade of experience in the industry, Ishika Menthol is committed to providing high-quality menthol products to its customers.

 

Our working objective is to achieve total customer satisfaction. At the same time, we also strive to offer effective and reliable products at most competitive prices within scheduled time. The driving force behind this enthusiastic approach is our quest to serve clients with the most dedicated and professionally.
